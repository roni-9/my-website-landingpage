Quick Fix Guides For XM Radio/Sirius Radio

Activate a New Radio

  1. Turn to preview channel (often Ch. 0/1) and note the Radio ID (ESN).
  2. Park outside with clear sky view for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Send a refresh from the provider’s site or app.
  4. Power‑cycle the radio and verify channels.

Refresh Your Signal

  1. With the engine on, keep the antenna unobstructed.
  2. Request a refresh hit (we’ll show you where).
  3. Leave the radio on the preview channel until it updates.

Streaming App Not Working

  1. Update the app and sign out/in.
  2. Clear cache (Android) or offload/reinstall (iOS).
  3. Check subscription status and simultaneous stream limits.

FAQs SiriusXM Radio — Activation, Billing, Streaming

How do I find my SiriusXM Radio ID (ESN)?
Tune to the preview/info channel of Sirius Radio/XM Radio (often 0 or 1) or check Settings ▶ About on the head unit. The ESN is usually 8–12 characters.
My Satellite Radio says “Check Antenna” or “No Signal”. What now?
Park in open sky for 10–15 minutes, ensure the antenna has a clear view, remove magnetic mounts from heated/metal‑coated glass, and route the cable away from interference. Then send a refresh.
Can I move my XM Satellite Radio subscription to a different car?
Yes. Most providers let you transfer your Sirius XM Subscription to a new ESN/Radio ID. Write down the old and new ESN/SiriusXM Radio ID first so you can switch without downtime.
Sirius XM Streaming app issues (buffering / can’t sign in)?
Update the app, toggle mobile data/Wi‑Fi, sign out/in, clear cache (Android) or offload/reinstall (iOS). Also check concurrent stream limits.
How do I cancel or renew my XM Satellite Radio ?
We explain options and the exact menu path. Official account actions must be done with the provider to keep your data safe.
